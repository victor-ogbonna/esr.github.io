About ESR-Nig.

Alleviating Technology Depravity in Nigeria by Developing STEM Students' skill-set in Embedded Systems and Robotics.

Nigeria, and indeed much of Africa, faces significant technological development challenges. ESR-Nig. aims to cultivate innovators capable of driving technological advancement from within Africa.

Our Vision Join Us

Our Vision & Objectives

ESR-Nig. is dedicated to fostering Nigerian technological innovation with solutions designed for African contexts.

Our Objectives:

  • Indigenous Technology: Developing locally manufactured electronics and smart devices
  • Advanced Mobility: Designing vehicles and systems for African terrain
  • Educational Transformation: Creating advanced labs for hands-on learning
