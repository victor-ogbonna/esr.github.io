Introduction

Alleviating Technology Depravity in Nigeria by Developing STEM Students' skill-set in Embedded Systems and Robotics.

We aim to empower Nigerian students to drive technological advancement by providing practical education and fostering innovation through hands-on experience with cutting-edge technologies.

Explore Our Projects

Our Vision

We envision a future where Nigeria becomes a global hub of technological innovation, producing homegrown solutions tailored to Africa's unique challenges through advanced robotics and embedded systems.

  • Indigenous Technology: Develop locally manufactured consumer electronics including smartphones, IoT devices, and smart home systems.
  • Advanced Mobility: Design and build next-generation vehicles, aircraft, and autonomous systems for African terrain.
  • Innovation Ecosystem: Foster a sustainable environment for technological entrepreneurship and problem-solving.
  • AI Integration: Pioneer artificial intelligence applications specifically designed for African contexts and challenges.

Our Comprehensive Curriculum

  • Foundational Courses: Electronics fundamentals, C/C++ programming, Python for robotics, and mechanical design basics.
  • Intermediate Skills: Microcontroller programming (ARM, AVR, PIC), sensor integration, IoT protocols, and real-time systems.
  • Advanced Topics: Machine learning for embedded systems, edge computing, FPGA development, and autonomous navigation.
  • Design & Simulation: Professional PCB design (Altium, KiCAD), 3D mechanical design (SolidWorks, Fusion 360), and system simulation.
  • Capstone Projects: Industry-sponsored projects, startup incubation, and commercial product development opportunities.
