Introduction

Alleviating Technology Depravity in Nigeria by Developing STEM Students' skill-set in Embedded Systems and Robotics.

We aim to empower Nigerian students to drive technological advancement by providing practical education and fostering innovation.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Nigeria becomes a hub of technological innovation, producing homegrown solutions tailored to Africa's unique challenges.

  • Develop indigenous consumer electronics like smartphones and smart devices.
  • Design and build vehicles, aircraft, and autonomous systems.
  • Foster innovation to address local and global technological challenges.

Our Comprehensive Curriculum

  • Foundational Courses: Electronics, C/C++ programming, and robotics basics.
  • Intermediate Skills Development: Microcontroller programming, sensors, IoT, and real-time systems.
  • Advanced Topics: AI integration, edge computing, FPGA, autonomous systems.
  • CAD and Design Integration: PCB design, 3D mechanical design.
  • Capstone Projects: Industry-based projects, internships, and entrepreneurship opportunities.
